Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

TV AppIcon does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Everything you do stays on your Mac.

Overview

TV AppIcon ("the App") is a macOS utility developed by Di Nerd Apps LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information. In short: it doesn't collect any.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identifiable information (name, email, address)
  • Images or files you process through the App
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Device identifiers or IP addresses
  • Crash reports or diagnostics sent to our servers

How the App Works

All image processing happens entirely on your local machine. When you drop an image into the App and export, the files are written directly to a folder you choose on your Mac. No data leaves your device at any point.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. It does not make any network requests except to load the in-app documentation pages hosted on GitHub Pages.

macOS App Sandbox

TV AppIcon runs in the macOS App Sandbox. It only accesses files in folders you explicitly select through the system file picker. It has no access to the rest of your file system.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. It is a developer tool and not directed at children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. Since the App collects no data, changes are unlikely to affect your privacy in any meaningful way.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at support@dinerdapps.com.
