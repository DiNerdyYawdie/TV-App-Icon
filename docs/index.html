How it works

1

Drop your source image

Drag any PNG or JPEG onto the app. Works best with a 1024×1024 image.

2

Optionally add layer images

Drop separate Front, Middle, and Back images to create a proper parallax icon stack.

3

Export and drag into Xcode

Choose an output folder. TV AppIcon exports everything, organized by slot and layer, ready for your asset catalog.

📋 Common Questions

What image size should I use?
A 1024×1024 PNG works best as your source image.

Do I need separate layer images?
No — if you only provide a source image, it will be used for all layers automatically.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?
Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchase".
